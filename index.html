Chai Tanet Co., Ltd. was established in 1991 by Mr.Viboon Rabiltossaporn who started the business by buying and selling auto parts and pickups building within the years a remarkable clients portfolio. With years of  experience and expertise in finding spare parts and products Mr. Viboon chased his vision to expand the business and singn long terms high profile collaborations working with The Royal Thai Army by Ordnance Department since 1989 still ongoing.


Products sold by the company to auction with The Royal Thai Army by Ordnance Department including the repair parts of war-fighting vehicles, UNIMOG 5 tons, the repair parts of war-fighting vehicles, UNIMOG 10 tons, 60 tons of unimog

Now the company is led by Mr. Sakka Rabiltossaporn as a second generation that came come into administration having took over after worked with his father for over 20 years, he has still contracts with the Royal Thai Army every year. And from the work of Chai Tanet Co., Ltd. is convinced that work honestly on time is the only path of success!
